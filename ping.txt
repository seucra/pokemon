# ⚡ Pokémon Craze: Roadmap & Future Features

## ✅ Completed Phases
- **Phase 1: The Architect (Team Builder)**
  - Full team slot management (6 slots).
  - Advanced type coverage matrix (Defensive Strategy).
  - Universal Pro-Grade Navigation (3-bar Sidebar Menu).
  - Premium Themes (GBA Retro & Obsidian Cinnabar Flare).
  - Interactive Pokédex explorer.

---

## 🏗️ Upcoming Features

### Phase 2: The Strategist (Data Hub)
- **Move Set Validation**: Ensure moves are legal for the selected Pokémon.
- **Export/Import**: Support for "Pokémon Showdown" format (Integrated in Settings).
- **IV/EV Calculator**: sliders and stat visualization for competitive builds.
- **Damage Calculator**: Simulate matchups between team members and common threats.

### Phase 3: The Trainer (Battle Engine)
- **Turn-Based Simulator**: Core logic for moves, abilities, and status effects.
- **AI Trainers**: Battle against preset NPCs of varying difficulty.
- **Sprite Animations**: Animated Gen 5 sprites and particle effects during battle.
- **Multiplayer Hub**: (Future) Real-time battles via WebSockets.
